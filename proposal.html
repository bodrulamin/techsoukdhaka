Overview

Executive Summary

TechSouk Dhaka specializes in fire safety products, HVAC systems, plumbing valves, and industrial pumps. This proposal outlines a comprehensive custom e-commerce platform designed specifically for your business needs.

The platform includes three main components: a powerful Admin Panel for business management, a Customer Web Application for online shopping, and a robust Backend API built with enterprise-grade Java Spring Boot.

Industry-Specific
Tailored for fire safety, HVAC, and industrial products with specialized categories and attributes.
Scalable Architecture

Multi-Platform
Web-based customer interface with optional mobile app support for iOS and Android.

Capabilities

Comprehensive Features

Admin Panel Features

Product Management
Full CRUD for products with variations, images, stock management, pricing, and categories. Support for fire extinguishers, valves, pumps, and all your product lines.
Category & Navigation
Hierarchical category structure for Fire Protection, HVAC, Plumbing, Pumps, and Accessories. Custom menu routing for easy navigation.
Order Management
Complete order lifecycle from placement to delivery. Order status tracking, charge calculations, and return processing.
Customer Management
Customer profiles, addresses, order history, wallet balances, and communication logs.
Delivery Management
Delivery man assignment, tracking, and commission management. Real-time delivery status updates.
City & Area Management
Geographic coverage configuration for Dhaka and beyond. Area-based delivery charges and service zones.
Outlet/Store Management
Multi-location inventory management. Store-wise product allocation and user permissions.
Coupon System
Create promotional coupons with minimum purchase requirements, usage limits, and validity periods.
Wallet System
Customer wallet management with add money, cashback, and coin-based rewards. Configurable earn and redeem thresholds.
Advertisement & Carousel
Homepage banner management for promotions and featured products. Advertisement placement and tracking.
Chat & Support
Real-time customer support chat system. Conversation history and message management.
Target & Challenges
Sales targets and challenges for delivery team with subscription tracking and performance metrics.
App Configuration
Centralized settings for delivery charges, payment gateways, business rules, and system parameters.
Permission Management
Role-based access control for admin users. Outlet-wise permissions and user management.
Stock Management
Inventory tracking with product variations. Low stock alerts and stock transfer between outlets.

Product Browsing

Referral System
